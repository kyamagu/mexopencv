MATLAB File Help: cv.ORB/ScaleFactor Index
cv.ORB/ScaleFactor

Pyramid decimation ratio.

greater than 1. ScaleFactor=2 means the classical pyramid, where each next level has 4x less pixels than the previous, but such a big scale factor will degrade feature matching scores dramatically. On the other hand, too close to 1 scale factor will mean that to cover certain scale range you will need more pyramid levels and so the speed will suffer. default 1.2
